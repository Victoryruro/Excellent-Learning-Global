Who We Are

Excellent Learning Global is a fast growing educational center committed to transforming lives through quality learning,virtue and excellence. With our base in Benson Idahosa University BIU, Benin City We are currently expanding operations to other institutions including Edo State University EDSU and Wellspring University. we had the oppurtunity to tutor over 1000+ students both online and onsite, impacting them with a touch of excellence and purpose. E_Learning Global kicked off operations in 2022 as an online educational platform and has grown to a fully registered company under the Nigeria government under the leadership of it's founder Shalom Uwaifo
